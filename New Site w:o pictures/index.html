Homeowners notice first

The water problems people usually feel before they know the cause.

These are often the first signs that something in the water needs attention. Each one can point to a different type of issue — and a different type of solution.

Hard Water & Scale

White spots, calcium buildup, cloudy shower glass, soap that does not work well, and long-term wear on plumbing and appliances.

Chlorine Smell & Bad Taste

Chemical smell at the tap, pool-like shower odor, unpleasant drinking water, and loss of confidence in what comes out of the faucet.

Skin & Hair Issues

Dry skin, rough hair, irritation, poor soap rinse, and everyday comfort problems that often trace back to water quality.
Solutions designed around your water

Explore the systems we install to solve different water problems in the home.

Each solution is designed for a specific type of water problem. Some homes need one system. Others need a combination, depending on the water source, plumbing layout, and what the homeowner wants to improve.

Whole House Filtration

Designed to improve water quality throughout the home by reducing chlorine, odor, and other unwanted contaminants before the water reaches showers, sinks, and appliances.

Water Softener

Designed to reduce hardness minerals that cause scale buildup, white spots, dry skin, rough hair, and reduced efficiency in plumbing and appliances.

Drinking Water System

Focused on the water you drink and cook with every day, using advanced filtration to improve taste and reduce dissolved contaminants.

Smart Leak Protection System

Helps homeowners detect water issues earlier, monitor system activity, and reduce risk from preventable leak-related damage.
About AquaRemedy

Water treatment built around real homes, real systems, and real service.

AquaRemedy is a water treatment company focused on solving real water problems with the right combination of testing, system design, installation, service, and repair.

We work with homeowners who are dealing with hard water, chlorine smell, bad taste, scale buildup, dry skin, rough hair, underperforming systems, or water equipment that needs professional attention.

We install and service a wide range of water treatment systems — including water softeners, backwashing filtration, drinking water systems, leak protection, and custom setups designed around the property.

We are familiar with equipment from brands such as US Water Systems, Chandler Systems, and other major water treatment manufacturers, but our approach is not tied to one brand only. We work on many types of systems and can help homeowners understand what actually fits their house and water conditions.

What we do:

  • whole house water treatment design and installation
  • water softener installation, service, and troubleshooting
  • drinking water and reverse osmosis systems
  • smart leak protection and monitoring
  • repair and maintenance of existing equipment
  • solutions for homes, laundry, offices, coffee shops, and light commercial use
