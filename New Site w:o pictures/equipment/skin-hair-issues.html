Skin & Hair Issues

Many homeowners don’t realize that their water can directly affect their skin and hair. Dry skin, irritation, and rough hair are often connected to what’s in the water.

Hard water and chlorine are the two most common reasons behind these issues.

  • dry or itchy skin after shower
  • hair feels rough or dull
  • soap doesn’t rinse properly
  • skin feels tight after washing

Why does water affect your skin and hair?

Hard water leaves mineral residue on your skin and hair. This makes it harder to rinse soap and shampoo completely.

Dry skin

Minerals and chlorine can strip natural oils from your skin.

Rough hair

Residue builds up on hair, making it feel dry and heavy.

Soap doesn’t work well

Hard water reduces lather and cleaning effectiveness.

Irritation

Chlorine can cause sensitivity and discomfort.

How to know if water is the problem?

If your skin feels dry right after showering or your hair never feels clean, your water may be the reason.

What is the solution?

Whole House Filtration + Water Softener

The best results usually come from removing chlorine and softening the water. This improves both comfort and overall water quality in the home.
