Skin & Hair Issues

Dry skin, rough hair, and irritation are often caused by your water — not your products.

  • dry or itchy skin after shower
  • hair feels rough or dull
  • soap doesn’t rinse properly
  • tight skin feeling after washing

Why water affects your skin and hair

Hard water leaves minerals on your skin and hair. Chlorine removes natural oils. Together, they create dryness and irritation.

Dry skin

Water strips natural oils and leaves residue.

Rough hair

Minerals build up and make hair feel heavy.

Soap doesn’t work

Hard water prevents proper lather.

Irritation

Chlorine can cause sensitivity.

What is the solution?

The best solution is combining a water softener with whole house filtration.
